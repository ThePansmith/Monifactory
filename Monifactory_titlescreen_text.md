# 0.13.0 is out!
**The biggest update in Moni history!**
Scroll down over this text for more info.
*Please make a backup before attempting to update from a 0.12 world, there are a lot of breaking changes!*

- A complete rework of the Microverse system.
  - New Microverse types will offer new challenges while expanding your possibilities to new limits.
  - Microverse Projectors received a major glow-up!
- Brand new multiblocks with never seen before mechanics.
  - Antimatter-based power, crafting with Color, or even powering your entire base remotely!
  - Everything you need to know is explained in the Quest Book.
- Many reworks and improvements across the entire pack.
  - Improved midgame progression flow.
  - Added alternate sources of Fission products.
  - Replaced Sterile Cleanroom with a special hatch.
  - Reworked Fusion Reactors.
- Greatly refined, content-rich endgame.
  - New materials and processing lines.
  - Reworked circuit themes.
  - New multiblocks with unique quirks.
  - Improved power balance with a brand new fuel option.
  - New MAX-tier components.
- Many balance changes.
- Brand new QoL features.
  - Pack mode switching directly available from the main menu!
  - Many mod updates bringing fixes and new QoL features.
- An absurd amount of bug fixes.
- An updated and fully reviewed Quest Book.
- And more!

From the entire Monifactory development team, **thank you for playing!** We hope you'll enjoy this update!
